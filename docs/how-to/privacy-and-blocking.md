## Blocking Users

### What Blocking Does

When you block someone:

**They CANNOT:**
- ❌ View your profile
- ❌ See your posts or stories
- ❌ Message you
- ❌ Comment on your content
- ❌ Subscribe to you
- ❌ Tip you
- ❌ Purchase content from you
- ❌ See you in search results
- ❌ Tag or mention you

**You CANNOT:**
- ❌ See their profile
- ❌ See their comments elsewhere
- ❌ Message them

**They DON'T Know:**
- 🤐 No notification they're blocked
- 🤐 Profile appears as if you deleted account
- 🤐 All past interactions hidden

### How to Block Someone

**Method 1: From Their Profile**

1. Go to user's profile
2. Click/tap menu icon (⋮ or •••)
3. Select **Block User**
4. Confirm: **Block**

**Method 2: From a Message**

1. Open message thread
2. Click/tap user's name or settings icon
3. Select **Block User**
4. Confirm

**Method 3: From a Comment**

1. Find their comment
2. Click/tap menu on comment (⋮)
3. Select **Block User**
4. Confirm

**Method 4: From Privacy Settings**

1. Settings → Privacy & Safety → Blocked Users
2. Click **Add User to Block List**
3. Enter username
4. Click **Block**

### Managing Blocked Users

**View Blocked List:**
```
Settings → Privacy & Safety → Blocked Users

BLOCKED USERS (12)

@username1 - Blocked 2 days ago
   [Unblock]

@username2 - Blocked 1 week ago
   [Unblock]

@username3 - Blocked 3 months ago
   [Unblock]
```

**Unblock a User:**
1. Find user in blocked list
2. Click **Unblock**
3. Confirm

**Note:** Unblocking restores their full access immediately.

## Restricting Users

### Restrict vs Block

**Restrict** = Softer approach
- They can still see your content
- But interactions are limited
- They don't know they're restricted

**Block** = Complete cutoff
- They can't see anything
- No interaction possible
- Your choice

### What Restricting Does

When you restrict someone:

**They CAN:**
- ✅ View your profile
- ✅ See your posts
- ✅ Remain subscribed

**BUT:**
- ⚠️ Their comments only visible to them (you can approve to show publicly)
- ⚠️ Their messages go to requests folder (not main inbox)
- ⚠️ You don't get notifications of their activity
- ⚠️ They can't see when you're online
- ⚠️ They can't see if you've read their messages

**Best For:**
- Overly chatty but not harmful users
- Clingy but paying subscribers
- Awkward situations (acquaintances)
- Testing before blocking

### How to Restrict Someone

1. Go to user's profile
2. Click menu icon (⋮)
3. Select **Restrict**
4. Confirm

**Unrestrict:**
Same process, select **Unrestrict**

## Geographic Restrictions

### Blocking by Location

Prevent users from specific regions from accessing your content.

**Common Uses:**
- Block your home country (privacy)
- Block where family/friends live
- Block prohibited jurisdictions
- Reduce unwanted attention from specific regions

### Setting Geographic Blocks

**Settings → Privacy & Safety → Geographic Restrictions**

```
BLOCK BY LOCATION

Block Entire Countries:
[+] Add Country

Currently Blocked:
🇺🇸 United States - [Remove]
🇨🇦 Canada - [Remove]
🇬🇧 United Kingdom - [Remove]

Block Specific Regions/States:
[+] Add Region

Currently Blocked:
📍 California, USA - [Remove]
📍 Texas, USA - [Remove]
```

**How It Works:**
- Based on IP address location
- Users see: "This profile is not available in your region"
- Applies to profile, all content, search results

**Important Notes:**
- VPNs can bypass geographic blocks
- Not 100% foolproof
- Combine with other privacy measures
- May reduce potential subscriber base

### Block Home Location

**Protect Local Privacy:**

```
MY LOCATION PRIVACY

My Location: [New York, USA ▼]

Block Radius:
( ) 10 miles
( ) 25 miles
(•) 50 miles
( ) 100 miles
( ) Custom

This blocks anyone whose IP shows they're within 50 miles of New York, USA.
```

**Use Case:**
"I don't want anyone who lives near me finding my content."

## Profile Privacy

### Public vs Private Account

**Public Account** (Default)
- ✅ Anyone can view profile
- ✅ Appears in search results
- ✅ Content previews visible
- ✅ Discoverable to new fans
- ✅ Better for growth

**Private Account**
- 🔒 Must request to follow
- 🔒 You approve each follower
- 🔒 Only followers see content
- 🔒 Hidden from search
- 🔒 Better for exclusive/closed communities

**Switch to Private:**

1. Settings → Privacy & Safety → Account Privacy
2. Toggle **Private Account** to ON
3. Confirm

**What Happens:**
- Current followers remain
- New people must request to follow
- You approve/deny each request
- Profile hidden from non-followers

## Personal Information Protection

### What to Never Share

**NEVER Share on Platform:**
- ❌ Real full name (use stage name)
- ❌ Home address
- ❌ Phone number
- ❌ Personal email (use separate creator email)
- ❌ Work location
- ❌ Family members' names
- ❌ Children's information
- ❌ School/university
- ❌ Specific neighborhood
- ❌ Car details (license plate, model)
- ❌ Daily routines/schedules
- ❌ Vacation dates (post after return)

### Identifying Information in Content

**Be Careful With:**

**Photos/Videos:**
- Remove EXIF data (GPS location)
- Watch reflections (windows showing street views)
- Cover mail/packages with addresses
- Hide prescription bottles (name/address)
- Blur license plates
- Remove location-specific landmarks

**Background Details:**
- Street signs
- Business names
- Unique architecture
- Distinctive decor
- School/work logos
- Name tags

**Tools to Help:**

```
METADATA REMOVAL

Settings → Privacy → Automatic Protections

☑ Strip EXIF data from photos
☑ Remove GPS location from uploads
☑ Remove metadata from videos
☑ Warn me if potential identifying info detected
```

## Safety Best Practices

### Creating Safe Boundaries

**Communication Boundaries:**

```
MY BOUNDARIES (Example)

✅ I WILL:
- Reply to messages within 24 hours
- Engage with comments
- Accept custom requests within my guidelines
- Be friendly and professional

❌ I WON'T:
- Meet in person
- Share personal contact info
- Move conversations off-platform
- Discuss my real identity
- Share my location
- Video call with subscribers
```

**Post This in Your Bio:**
> "I don't meet fans in person, share personal contact info, or chat off-platform. Please respect these boundaries! 💕"

### Recognizing Red Flags

**Warning Signs - Consider Blocking:**

🚩 **Asks for personal information repeatedly**
> "What's your real name?" "Where do you live?" "What's your phone number?"

🚩 **Wants to move off-platform**
> "Add me on WhatsApp" "Let's chat on Telegram" "Here's my phone number"

🚩 **Pushes boundaries**
> "Just one meet-up" "I'll pay extra for your number" "Send me a real photo"

🚩 **Obsessive behavior**
> Messages 50 times/day, mentions you constantly, tracks your online status

🚩 **Threatening or aggressive**
> "You'll regret this" "I know where you live" "I'll find you"

🚩 **Financial manipulation**
> "I'll subscribe if you..." "Send me free content first" "I'll tip big next time"

🚩 **Tries to doxx you**
> Posts your real name, shares your location, threatens to expose you

**ACTION:** Block immediately, report to platform, document everything.

## Emergency Situations

### If You're Being Harassed

**Immediate Steps:**

1. **Block the user** immediately
2. **Screenshot everything** (messages, comments, posts)
3. **Report to platform** (Settings → Help → Report Harassment)
4. **Do not engage** with the harasser
5. **Document timeline** of events

**Report Format:**

```
HARASSMENT REPORT

User: @username
Date started: [Date]
Type: [Threatening messages / Doxxing / etc.]
Attachments: [Screenshots]
Additional info: [Description]

I am requesting:
☑ User banned from platform
☑ Content removed
☑ IP ban
```

### If You're Being Stalked

**Serious Situation - Take Action:**

1. **Block on all platforms** (not just this one)
2. **Report to platform immediately**
3. **Contact local police** (online stalking is a crime)
4. **Document everything** (dates, times, screenshots)
5. **Consider legal action** (restraining order)
6. **Pause content** if you feel unsafe
7. **Get support** (friends, family, counselor)

**Resources:**
- National stalking helpline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- Cyber Crimes division of FBI: ic3.gov
- Platform safety team: safety@BoyFanz.com

### If Personal Info Is Leaked (Doxxing)

**Damage Control:**

1. **Report to platform** immediately (emergency priority)
2. **Contact site admin** to remove leaked info
3. **File police report** (doxxing is illegal)
4. **Google yourself** - request removal of leaked info
5. **Change passwords** on all accounts
6. **Enable 2FA** everywhere
7. **Monitor credit** if financial info leaked
8. **Consider legal action**

**Platform Will:**
- Remove doxxing content immediately
- Ban user permanently
- Preserve evidence for law enforcement
- Support your legal actions

## Account Recovery & Security

### If Your Account Is Hacked

**Signs of Hacking:**
- Login from unknown locations
- Posts you didn't create
- Messages you didn't send
- Payout information changed
- Unknown users blocked
- Settings changed

**Immediate Response:**

1. **Change password immediately** (if you still can)
2. **Contact platform support** (priority: "Account Hacked")
3. **Enable 2FA** if not already on
4. **Review login activity**
5. **Check payout information**
6. **Scan for malware** on your devices
7. **Review content** posted during hack

**Platform Support:**
```
Subject: URGENT - Account Hacked

My account @username has been compromised.

Evidence:
- Unauthorized posts on [dates]
- Unknown login from [location] on [date]
- Messages sent I didn't write
- Payout info changed

Please:
1. Freeze account immediately
2. Remove unauthorized content
3. Restore account to me
4. Preserve hack evidence
5. Ban unauthorized access

I can verify identity with: [ID, original email, etc.]
```

## Family & Friends Privacy

### Keeping Content Private from Known People

**Multi-Layer Approach:**

**1. Geographic Blocking**
- Block your city/region
- Block where family lives
- 50-100 mile radius

**2. Search Engine Blocking**
- Disable Google indexing
- Won't appear in searches
- Harder to stumble upon

**3. Stage Name Only**
- Never use real name
- Different appearance
- Alter voice if needed
- No identifying tattoos/marks visible

**4. Social Media Separation**
- Different photos
- Don't cross-link accounts
- Separate email addresses
- Different phone number

**5. Content Boundaries**
- No showing your face (optional)
- No identifying locations
- No personal details
- Generic background

**"Stealth Mode" Checklist:**

- [ ] Geographic blocks enabled (home area)
- [ ] Using complete stage name
- [ ] Separate email for platform
- [ ] No face showing (or masked/different makeup)
- [ ] Search engine indexing OFF
- [ ] No identifiable background
- [ ] Generic bio (no personal details)
- [ ] Social media accounts not linked
- [ ] Different phone number for 2FA
- [ ] Never mentioned platform on personal accounts
