# Dashboard Guide - Your Command Center

## Overview
Your dashboard is the heart of your BoyFanz experience. It provides real-time insights into your performance, earnings, and fan engagement.

---

## Understanding Your Dashboard

### 💰 Revenue Card
**What it shows:**
- Total earnings to date
- Growth percentage vs. last 30 days  
- Trend indicator (up/down arrow)

**What to do:**
- Click the card to see detailed breakdown
- View earnings by day, week, or month
- Export reports for tax purposes

💡 **Pro Tip:** Check your revenue daily to spot trends early!

---

### 👥 Active Fans Card
**What it shows:**
- Current active subscribers
- New fans this week
- Growth rate

**What to do:**
- Click to see full fan list
- Sort by engagement, spending, or join date
- Identify your top supporters

💡 **Pro Tip:** Message your top fans personally - they love the attention!

---

### 👀 Content Views Card
**What it shows:**
- Total views across all your content
- Engagement rate increase/decrease
- Most popular content type

**What to do:**
- Click to see which content performs best
- Double down on what works
- Adjust strategy for underperforming content

💡 **Pro Tip:** Content posted 8-10 PM gets 40% more views on average!

---

### ⏰ Pending Reviews Card
**What it shows:**
- Content waiting for moderation approval
- Estimated review time
- Rejection notifications

**What to do:**
- Check daily for approvals
- Review rejection reasons if applicable
- Re-upload corrected content quickly

💡 **Pro Tip:** Content typically reviews in 2-4 hours. Weekends may be slower.

## Troubleshooting

### "Revenue not updating"
**Solution:** Refresh the page. Revenue updates every 15 minutes.

### "Content stuck in pending"
**Solution:** Wait 2-4 hours. If still pending after 24hrs, contact support.

### "Can't see recent uploads"
**Solution:** Click "View All" to see complete library. Dashboard shows last 3 only.

### "Dashboard loading slowly"
**Solution:**
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
