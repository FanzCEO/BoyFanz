**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## Your Rights as a Subscriber

At FANZ, we believe in transparency. When you subscribe to a creator, you deserve to know exactly what you're getting.

---

## What Your Subscription Includes

Every subscription on FANZ includes:

✅ **Subscription-Tier Content** - Posts marked as "Subscribers Only"
✅ **Direct Messaging** - Ability to message the creator
✅ **Priority Interactions** - Your comments and messages are highlighted
✅ **Early Access** - First to see new subscription content

## Creator Requirements

All FANZ creators must maintain:

- **Minimum 70%** of content accessible to subscribers
- **Maximum 30%** pay-per-view content
- **Limited promotional messages** (max 2 PPV solicitations per week)

Creators who don't meet these standards face account restrictions.

Look for this badge when choosing creators:

🏆 **Subscription Friendly**

Creators with this badge maintain:
- 90%+ content accessible to subscribers
- Less than 10% PPV content
- Zero compliance issues

## Your Protections

### 1. Pre-Subscription Disclosure

Before any subscription purchase, you'll see:
- Exactly what's included
- The creator's content ratio
- Clear information about any additional purchases

You must acknowledge this disclosure before subscribing.

### 2. Content Ratio Guarantee

If a creator's actual content ratio is **significantly different** (>20%) from what was shown when you subscribed, you may request a refund.

### 3. Message Limits

Creators cannot send more than **2 promotional PPV messages per week**. If you're receiving excessive solicitations, report the creator.

### 4. Complaint Process

If you feel misled, you can file a complaint:
1. Go to the creator's profile
2. Click "Report Issue"
3. Select "Misleading Content Ratio"
4. Describe your concern

We investigate all complaints within 24-48 hours.

---

## Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund within **48 hours** of subscribing if:

| Reason | Eligible |
|--------|----------|
| Content ratio differs >20% from disclosure | ✅ Yes |
| Excessive PPV solicitation messages | ✅ Yes |
| Creator's content style changed dramatically | ✅ Case-by-case |
| You simply changed your mind | ❌ No |
| You didn't like the content quality | ❌ No |

### How to Request a Refund

1. Go to Settings → Subscriptions
2. Find the subscription
3. Click "Request Refund"
4. Select your reason
5. Submit

Refund requests are reviewed within 48 hours.

---

## What We Don't Guarantee

To be clear, subscriptions do **NOT** guarantee:

- Any specific number of posts per week/month
- Response to every message you send
- Access to all content a creator has ever posted
- Personal interaction beyond what the creator offers

Content availability and posting frequency vary by creator.

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

Be cautious of creators who:

⚠️ Post mostly "teaser" content with links to paid items
⚠️ Send frequent messages asking you to buy PPV
⚠️ Have a high PPV ratio (check before subscribing)
⚠️ Promise things not shown in our official disclosure
