# FANZ Platform Compliance Policies

**Effective Date:** February 4, 2026
**Applies To:** All FANZ Platforms

---

## 1. AGE VERIFICATION POLICY

### User Age Requirements
- All users must be 18 years of age or older
- Age verification required before accessing adult content
- False age declarations result in immediate account termination

### Creator Age Requirements
- All creators must be 18 years of age or older
- Government-issued photo ID required during registration
- ID must show date of birth and photo
- Re-verification may be required periodically

### Depicted Individuals
- All individuals depicted in content must be 18+
- Creator must verify age and obtain consent from ALL depicted individuals
- Consent documentation must be retained

---

## 2. CONTENT CONSENT POLICY

### Required Consent
Before uploading any content depicting other individuals, creators must:

1. **Verify Identity** - Confirm the identity of all depicted individuals
2. **Verify Age** - Confirm all depicted individuals are 18+
3. **Obtain Written Consent** - Get signed consent form
4. **Maintain Records** - Keep consent documentation for 7 years

### Consent Form Requirements
- Full legal name of depicted individual
- Date of birth
- Government ID number
- Explicit consent for distribution
- Signature and date
- Description of content covered

### Consent Revocation
- Depicted individuals may revoke consent at any time
- Revocation requests processed within 48 hours
- Content removed from all platforms upon revocation

## 3. PROHIBITED CONTENT POLICY

### Absolutely Prohibited (Zero Tolerance)

| Category | Description | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| CSAM | Any sexual content depicting minors | Immediate termination, law enforcement referral |
| Non-consensual | Content shared without consent | Removal, possible termination |
| Trafficking | Content related to sex trafficking | Immediate termination, law enforcement referral |
| Bestiality | Content depicting animal abuse | Immediate termination |
| Non-consensual deepfakes | AI-generated intimate images without consent | Removal within 48 hours, termination |
| Incest (real) | Content depicting real incestuous acts | Immediate termination |
| Extreme violence | Real violence, gore, death | Immediate termination |

### Content Requiring Review

- Roleplay content with age-related themes
- BDSM content (must show clear consent)
- Content with weapons
- Content depicting intoxication

### Reporting Prohibited Content
Report to: support@fanzunlimited.com
Response time: 24-48 hours

## 4. PAYWALL TRANSPARENCY POLICY

### Content Ratio Requirements
- Minimum 70% of content must be accessible to subscribers
- Maximum 30% pay-per-view (PPV) content
- Ratios calculated on rolling 90-day basis

### Disclosure Requirements
- Pre-subscription disclosure of content breakdown
- Clear labeling of PPV vs. subscription content
- No misleading marketing claims

### Prohibited Marketing Phrases
- "Full access"
- "Unlimited access"
- "All content included"
- "Everything included"
- "Complete access"

### Enforcement
- Warning at 25% PPV ratio
- Restrictions at 40% PPV ratio
- Account suspension for repeated violations

---

## 5. MESSAGE & SOLICITATION POLICY

### PPV Solicitation Limits
- Maximum 2 broadcast PPV messages per week
- Individual messages monitored for spam patterns
- No misleading subject lines

### Prohibited Messaging Practices
- Impersonation by third-party "chatters"
- Automated responses without disclosure
- Spam or excessive promotional messages
- Threatening or harassing messages

### Chatter/Assistant Policy
If using assistants to respond to messages:
- Must be disclosed in profile
- Assistant cannot impersonate creator
- Creator remains responsible for all content

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY POLICY

### Copyright Ownership
- Creators retain copyright to their original content
- Platform has license to display and distribute content
- Creators may not upload content they don't own or have rights to

### DMCA Compliance
- DMCA agent: dmca@fanzunlimited.com
- Takedown requests processed within 48 hours
- Counter-notice process available
- Repeat infringer policy in effect

### Reporting Copyright Infringement
Email dmca@fanzunlimited.com with:
- Description of copyrighted work
- URL of infringing content
- Your contact information
- Statement of good faith
- Signature (electronic acceptable)

## 10. ENFORCEMENT & APPEALS

### Violation Process

1. **First Violation:** Warning (most cases)
2. **Second Violation:** Temporary restriction
3. **Third Violation:** Extended suspension
4. **Severe/Repeated:** Permanent termination

### Zero Tolerance Violations
Immediate termination without warning for:
- CSAM
- Sex trafficking content
- Real violence
- Repeated serious violations

### Appeals Process
1. Email admin@fanzunlimited.com within 30 days
2. Include account details and explanation
3. Response within 7 business days
4. Decision is final
