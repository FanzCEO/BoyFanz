# FANZ Creator Content & Monetization Policy

**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026
**Policy Version:** 1.0
**Applies To:** All FANZ Platforms

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes mandatory requirements for content monetization to ensure subscribers receive fair value and accurate expectations. Violations may result in account restrictions or termination.

---

## The Golden Rule

**Subscribers must receive what they're promised.**

If you advertise "exclusive content access" with a subscription, the majority of your content must be accessible to subscribers without additional purchases.

---

## Content Ratio Requirements

### Minimum Standards

| Content Type | Minimum Ratio | Maximum Ratio |
|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| Subscription-Accessible | **70%** | No limit |
| Pay-Per-View (PPV) | No minimum | **30%** |
| Free/Public | No minimum | No limit |

### How Ratios Are Calculated

- Based on posts from the **last 90 days**
- All post types count equally (photos, videos, text)
- Ratios are calculated daily and displayed on your profile

### Ratio Examples

**Compliant Creator (Good):**
- 80 subscription posts
- 10 free posts
- 10 PPV posts
- Total: 90% accessible, 10% PPV ✅

**Non-Compliant Creator (Bad):**
- 50 subscription posts
- 5 free posts
- 45 PPV posts
- Total: 55% accessible, 45% PPV ❌

---

## Pay-Per-View (PPV) Rules

### Posting Limits

1. **Ratio Limit:** PPV cannot exceed 30% of your total content
2. **Frequency Limit:** Maximum 1 PPV post per 5 subscription posts
3. **Weekly Limit:** System tracks and may restrict excessive PPV posting

### PPV Messaging Limits

To protect subscribers from excessive promotional messages:

| Message Type | Weekly Limit |
|--------------|--------------|
| Broadcast PPV promotions | **2 per week** |
| Mass DM solicitations | **2 per week** |
| Individual PPV offers | Unlimited (but monitored) |

### What Counts as PPV

- Posts marked as "Pay-Per-View"
- Locked content requiring additional purchase
- "Tip to unlock" content
- Premium DM content for sale

---

## Prohibited Marketing Practices

### Banned Phrases

The following phrases are **PROHIBITED** in:
- Profile bios
- Subscription descriptions
- Marketing materials
- Promotional messages

| Prohibited | Why | Use Instead |
|------------|-----|-------------|
| "Full access" | Implies all content included | "Subscription content access" |
| "Unlimited access" | Misleading if PPV exists | "Subscriber benefits" |
| "All content" | False if PPV exists | "Subscription content" |
| "Everything included" | Deceptive | "Subscription tier access" |
| "No additional charges" | False if PPV exists | "Subscription included" |
| "Complete access" | Misleading | "Subscriber access" |
| "All exclusive content" | Implies everything | "Exclusive subscriber content" |

### Automated Detection

Our system automatically scans for prohibited phrases. Content containing these phrases will be:
1. **Blocked** from posting
2. **Flagged** with a suggestion for compliant language
3. **Logged** for compliance monitoring

## Compliance Status Levels

### Level 1: Compliant ✅
- PPV ratio under 25%
- No recent warnings
- Eligible for "Subscription Friendly" badge (at 90%+ accessible content)

### Level 2: Warning ⚠️
- PPV ratio between 25-40%
- Reduced visibility in search/discovery
- Email notification sent
- Must improve within 14 days

### Level 3: Restricted 🚫
- PPV ratio exceeds 40%
- **Cannot accept new subscribers**
- Removed from discovery/recommendations
- Must reduce PPV ratio to restore account

### Level 4: Review 🔍
- Pattern of violations detected
- Manual review by compliance team
- May result in account termination

---

## Enforcement Actions

### First Violation
- Warning email
- Dashboard notification
- 14 days to correct

### Second Violation
- Visibility reduced by 50%
- Removed from "Featured" sections
- 7 days to correct

### Third Violation
- New subscriptions disabled
- Existing subscribers notified
- Must appeal to restore

### Severe/Repeated Violations
- Account suspension
- Possible permanent ban
- Payout hold pending review

---

## Subscriber Refund Rights

Subscribers may request a refund if:

1. Creator's actual content ratio differs by **>20%** from displayed ratio at time of subscription
2. Excessive PPV solicitation (>5 messages per week)
3. Content dramatically changes after subscription

Refund requests are reviewed within 48 hours.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I ever have more than 30% PPV content?**
A: No. This is a hard limit to protect subscriber trust.

**Q: What if I'm a new creator with few posts?**
A: New creators have a grace period. Ratios are calculated once you have 10+ posts.

**Q: Can I appeal a compliance decision?**
A: Yes. Email compliance@fanz.network with your case.

**Q: Do tips count as PPV?**
A: No. Tips are voluntary and don't lock content.

**Q: What about "teaser" posts that link to PPV?**
A: These count as subscription content if the teaser itself is viewable.
